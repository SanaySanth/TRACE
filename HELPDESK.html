Frequently Asked Questions

Is TRACE capable of solving cybercrimes?

Yes, TRACE has specially designed tools for cybercrime investigations.

Is TRACE used in every part of India?

TRACE is being implemented step by step in various states and regions.

Who can TRACE use?

TRACE is intended for professional use by approved law enforcement agencies, crime scene experts, and government agencies. TRACE is a secure system intended to be beneficial to professionals working in crime investigation and public safety.

Does TRACE enable various agencies to collaborate?

Yes. TRACE encourages coordination among various enforcement and investigation units. It enables them to exchange information, join cases, and prevent duplication of effort.

What is the expected effect on conviction rates of TRACE?

Through simplification of investigation and greater inter-agency coordination, TRACE will be expected to substantially boost conviction rates.
