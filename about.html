About TRACE

TRACE (Tactical Real-time Analysis of Criminal Events) is an initiative under the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, aimed at modernizing criminal investigation through data-driven insights, real-time tracking, and forensic analysis. TRACE integrates national crime records, live incident feeds, cybercrime investigation modules, and ballistics forensics into a centralized dashboard for rapid and tactical decision-making. With TRACE, investigative agencies are empowered with automated analytics, inter-agency coordination tools, and live crime heatmaps, helping reduce response time and increasing conviction rates effectively across the nation.

Services Provided by TRACE

  • Real-Time Crime Monitoring: Live feed and alert generation for ongoing criminal activities across zones and states.
  • Data Analytics: Predictive analysis and statistical breakdown of crime trends based on demographics, location, and type.
  • Cyber Crime Investigation: Specialized portals for cyber offenses including digital forensics and evidence preservation.
  • Ballistics Analysis: Integration with forensic labs for rapid ballistic matching and case linkage.
  • Centralized Case Management: Single-window platform for managing ongoing, past, and reopened criminal cases.
  • Forensics and DNA Matching: Advanced forensic services with AI-assisted DNA and fingerprint matching systems.
